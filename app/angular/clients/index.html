As a client on liveeverfit.com, there are several subscription options and involvement levels available for you, the trainer, nutritionist or instructor. And, based on the level of commitment you decide, your online coaching will be as follows:

Tier 1:

  • Access to our entire storefront.

  • Customized Diet/Fitness workouts on a Once Per Month consulting basis.

Tier 4:

  • Customized Meal plans, Fitness workouts on a BI-WEEKLY consulting basis (twice per week).

Tier 5:

  • Competition Prep: Customized meal plans, progress updates, and workout changes all on a continuous AS NEEDED basis.
