what do i owe my clients?

As a fitness professional, your friends, family and clientele look up to you for guidance through the vast amount of fitness information. The more active you are on your blog, and client interactions, ultimately the more successful you will be. As a client on www.liveeverfit.com, there are several options of subscription and levels of involvement from you, the trainer or nutritionist.

As a promoter, your audience will all be Tier 1 subscribers that will interact with you via messaging and blog comments. Share and grow your following and income by sharing the link via the “INVITE” button on your profile page.

The subscriptions levels for our EverFit clients are below:

Tier 1:

Access to our entire storefront, access to all fitness videos for all trainers, access to the entire store and to purchase items, and ability to receive email updates and promotions.

Tier 2:

Customized Diet/Fitness workouts on a Once Per Month consulting basis. Access to trainer/nutritionist contact info. INITIAL phone consult, and email access to trainer or nutritionist.

Tier 3:

Customized Diet/Fitness workouts on a Once Per Week consulting basis. Access to trainer/nutritionist contact info. As well as WEEKLY phone consult, and access to trainer/ nutritionist contact info, and email access.

Tier 4:

Customized Meal plans, Fitness workouts on a BI WEEKLY consulting basis (twice per week). As well as BI WEEKLY phone consult, and access to trainer/ nutritionist contact info, and email access.

Tier 5:

Competition Prep: Customized meal plans, progress updates and work out changes, all on a continuous, AS NEEDED basis. As needed and no limit to phone consultations in order to get the Client in Contest condition, trainer/nutritionist contact info and email access.

Please take the time to understand the different levels of involvement as a trainer/nutritionist with your new clients. You can turn ON or OFF your online Training ability at any time inside of your Settings.
